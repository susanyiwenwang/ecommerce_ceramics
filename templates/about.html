About the artist CT Kao

Since 2007, Chien-Tzu Kao has immersed herself in the world of ceramics. She has experimented with colors, natural textures and organic shapes. Her ceramics pieces are as individual as a live being.
A mother of two grown musician daughters, she has found herself a new passion for ceramics and painting, placing the care of motherhood into the creation of these individual pieces.
